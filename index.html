How do we look when we talk about love?

“It’s actually funny because I just got to get coffee with someone I love who I haven’t seen in a while. Thinking about love especially on this sunny day is nice”
"I love my dog. he’s my best friend in the world. I’m getting giddy just talking about him. He’ll lick my face and he’ll scratch me… and I can tell him anything."
"They show up in my dreams sometimes, and I’m like ‘I love you so much’ but you’ll never know cause you can’t cause your lost in a sea of people and you’re lost in the sea of yourself"
"I love my mom… She came from Taiwan and she didn’t go to college so I’m actually first generation. I always worry about trying to meet her expectations, but she’s always saying just do whatever you want and have fun in college. But I can’t because I feel like I need to make her proud."
"She's very resilient... but you'd never know it just talking to her. She has had so many issues throughout her life that I've had the fortune to hear about because she has trusted me with them."
"I love her because I think she’s turning into a really good person, and i’m proud to be her older brother."
